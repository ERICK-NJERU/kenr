Co-Curricular Activities

Life at Kenrama

Education extends well beyond textbooks. We believe a well-rounded learner is one who participates, explores, and grows in every aspect of school life.

Physical Development

Sports & Athletics

Sports are an integral part of life at Kenrama. Our learners participate in a variety of team and individual sports that develop physical fitness, teamwork, leadership, and resilience.

  • Football (Boys & Girls)
  • Volleyball & Netball
  • Athletics & Track Events
  • Rope Skipping & Indoor Games
Interest Groups

Clubs & Societies

Kenrama's clubs provide learners with spaces to explore their passions, develop new skills, and connect with peers who share their interests — an important part of the CBC holistic development goal.

  • Environmental / Wildlife Club
  • Journalism & Debate Club
  • Science & Technology Club
  • Young Farmers Club
Creative Arts

Music & Drama

Creative expression is a cornerstone of our co-curriculum. Our music and drama programs allow learners to discover their artistic voices while building confidence, communication, and cultural appreciation.

  • School Choir & Vocal Groups
  • Drama & Theatre Performances
  • Traditional Music & Dance
  • Annual School Concert
Character Formation

Spiritual Growth

At Kenrama, we believe that moral and spiritual development are inseparable from academic growth. We nurture learners to be morally upright, compassionate, and responsible members of society.

  • Daily Morning Devotions
  • Religious Education (CRE / IRE)
  • Character & Values Programs
  • Community Service Initiatives

Be Part of Something Great

Join the Kenrama family and give your child an experience that goes beyond academics.
