How To Apply

Admissions Process

Enrolling your child at Kenrama Education Centre is a straightforward four-step process. We are here to guide you every step of the way.

01

Visit the School

Schedule a visit to our school in Nkubu, Meru County. Meet our administration team, tour the facilities, and get a feel for the Kenrama environment and community.

02

Fill Admission Form

Collect and complete our admission application form. Submit it to the school office along with the required supporting documents listed below.

03

Assessment (If Required)

Depending on the grade level and availability, learners may undergo a brief placement assessment. This helps us place your child in the most appropriate class to support their learning journey.

04

Admission & Orientation

Upon successful assessment, your child is formally admitted. We conduct a brief orientation to help new learners settle in comfortably and begin their journey at Kenrama Education Centre.

Required Documents

  • Child's Original Birth Certificate
  • Previous School Report / Progress Card
  • 2 Recent Passport-Size Photographs
  • Parent / Guardian National ID Copy
  • Transfer Letter (for transferring learners)
