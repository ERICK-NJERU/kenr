What We Provide

Our Infrastructure

Accommodation

Boarding Facility

Kenrama offers safe, comfortable boarding facilities for both boys and girls. Our dormitories are well-supervised, clean, and designed to give learners a structured, supportive environment. Boarding helps learners focus on academics away from distractions, with organized study time, meals, and activities.

Transport

School Buses (×2)

The school operates two buses providing safe and reliable transport for learners within surrounding areas of Nkubu. Our buses are maintained to the highest safety standards and operated by trained, responsible drivers — giving parents peace of mind every school day.

Technology

Computer Laboratory

Our fully equipped computer lab provides learners with hands-on digital literacy skills as required by CBC. Learners access computers for research, coding basics, and digital communication — building skills essential for Kenya's growing digital economy and future careers.

Learning Resources

Library

Kenrama's library is stocked with CBC-aligned textbooks, reference materials, novels, and educational resources across all subjects. It fosters a culture of independent reading and research, with a quiet and comfortable space for learners to study and explore knowledge beyond the classroom.

Recreation

Playground & Sports Grounds

Physical activity is central to holistic development. Our playground and sports grounds provide space for football, athletics, volleyball, netball, and other team sports — keeping learners active, healthy, and developing essential teamwork and discipline skills.

Nutrition

Dining Hall

Our dining hall provides nutritious, well-balanced meals for boarding learners and day scholars. We understand that proper nutrition is fundamental to academic performance and physical well-being, and we are committed to providing healthy, adequate meals every day.

Experience Our Campus

Visit Kenrama Education Centre in person to see our facilities. We welcome prospective parents and learners to tour our campus.
