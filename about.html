Our Story

Who Is Kenrama?

Kenrama Education Centre is a private, mixed day and boarding school situated in Nkubu, Meru County, Kenya. Committed to Kenya's Competency Based Curriculum (CBC), we offer quality education from Pre-Primary (PP1) through Junior Secondary (Grade 9).

Our institution is built on a culture of discipline, purpose, and academic excellence. We believe every child holds unique potential, and our role is to provide the environment, resources, and guidance to help them realise it fully.

With boarding facilities for both boys and girls, two school buses serving the surrounding areas, a computer lab, library, playground, and dining hall — Kenrama is equipped to support every dimension of a learner's development.
