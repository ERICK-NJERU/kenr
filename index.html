Our Programmes

Everything Your Child Needs to Excel

From modern classrooms to boarding facilities and school buses — Kenrama provides a full, supportive learning environment.

CBC Academics

Kenya's forward-looking Competency Based Curriculum from Pre-Primary through Junior Secondary, taught by qualified and caring teachers.

Boarding Facility

Safe, comfortable, and well-supervised boarding for boys and girls — a true home away from home with meals and structured study time.

School Transport

Two dedicated school buses providing safe, reliable transport for learners within the surrounding areas of Nkubu every school day.

Computer Lab

A fully equipped computer laboratory preparing learners for the digital economy with hands-on technology skills.

Library

A well-stocked library that fosters a culture of reading, independent research, and academic curiosity beyond the classroom.

Sports & Activities

Structured sports, music, drama, and clubs that develop the whole learner and build teamwork, leadership, and confidence.

Who We Are

A School Built on Purpose, Discipline & Faith

Kenrama Education Centre is a well-established private school located in Nkubu, Meru County. We offer CBC-aligned education from PP1 through Junior Secondary (Grade 9), in a safe, structured, and caring environment where every child is valued.

Our approach blends strong academics with character formation — shaping young people who are disciplined, morally upright, and ready for tomorrow's opportunities. Parents trust us because we treat every learner as our own.
