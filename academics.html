Our Approach

Competency Based Curriculum (CBC)

At Kenrama, we fully implement Kenya's Competency Based Curriculum — a learner-centred approach that focuses on developing skills, values, and competencies rather than rote memorisation.

CBC equips our learners with critical thinking, creativity, communication, and collaboration skills — preparing them to thrive in an ever-changing world and Kenyan economy.

Level 1

Pre-Primary School

PP1 & PP2 · Ages 4–6

Our Pre-Primary program lays the critical foundation for a child's lifelong learning journey. Through play-based and inquiry-driven activities, learners develop cognitive, social, emotional, and physical skills in a nurturing environment.

LEARNING AREAS
  • Language Activities (Mother Tongue & English)
  • Mathematical Activities
  • Environmental Activities
  • Psychomotor & Creative Activities
  • Religious Education & Life Skills
Level 2

Lower Primary School

Grade 1, 2 & 3 · Ages 6–9

Lower Primary builds literacy, numeracy, and foundational competencies. Learners are guided through a rich and structured curriculum that encourages curiosity, creativity, and independent thinking.

LEARNING AREAS
  • Literacy (English & Kiswahili)
  • Mathematics
  • Environmental & Social Studies
  • Christian / Islamic Religious Education
  • Movement & Creative Arts
Level 3

Upper Primary School

Grade 4, 5 & 6 · Ages 9–12

Upper Primary deepens the learner's academic breadth across core and elective subjects. Special emphasis is placed on STEM, digital literacy, and critical thinking — skills essential for the modern Kenyan economy.

LEARNING AREAS
  • English Language & Literature
  • Kiswahili
  • Mathematics
  • Integrated Science
  • Social Studies
  • Agriculture & Nutrition
  • Creative Arts & Sports
  • Religious Education
Level 4

Junior Secondary School

Grade 7, 8 & 9 · Ages 12–15

Junior Secondary is a transformative stage within Kenya's CBC pathway. Kenrama delivers a rich, multi-disciplinary curriculum that develops the learner's specialised interests, career awareness, and advanced academic skills.

LEARNING AREAS
  • English Language
  • Kiswahili / Kenya Sign Language
  • Mathematics
  • Integrated Science
  • Health Education
  • Pre-Technical & Pre-Career Education
  • Social Studies
  • Religious Education
  • Business Studies
  • Agriculture
  • Creative Arts & Sports
